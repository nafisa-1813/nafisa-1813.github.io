About

I am a Data Scientist at Deloitte and hold an M.S. in Data Science from Columbia University's Data Science Institute, where I was also a University Life Ambassador and Executive Secretary of the Columbia Consulting Association.

My technical work has spanned scalable ML pipelines for battery state-of-health estimation, NLP automation for pharmaceutical authoring workflows, and EEG signal processing for stress and emotion detection. I am drawn to problems where rigorous mathematics meets real-world constraints.

Outside of ML, I care about the structural conditions that allow people — especially women — to participate fully in science and engineering. I was featured by Columbia's Data Science Institute for Women's History Month, speaking about engineering and women's right to education and healthcare.

I write about AI — its architecture, its safety, its social consequences — because the people building these systems have an obligation to think carefully about what they are actually making.
